What We do

Today's global economy is unpredictable and characterised by constant change. Organisations that want to thrive must be adept at adapting. They need to strategically transform by implementing and embedding new business models that strengthen their position to gain competitive advantage, and affirm stakeholder confidence. Doing nothing is not an option. Long-term success depends on the delivery of transformation initiatives and improving overall organisational agility through the discipline of change management. BOA Change Management Services provides organisations with a structured approach for moving from current to target state with a thorough and smooth implementation that ensures lasting benefits

Our Mission

To enable long-lasting transformative change by improving
delivery methods across the public and private sectors.

Public Sector

Infrastructure Governance - implement frameworks,
standards and processes needed to ensure that
infrastructure delivers the intended outcomes required to
achieve economic growth and development.

Private Sector

Organisational Change Management - design
organisations in such a way as to enable continuous
adaptation to an ever-evolving environment.

Objectives

Our practice is based on three core objectives.

Strengthen Governance

Promote and embed a culture that encourages thorough analysis of the viability of projects before approval to start in order to utilize finite resources efficiently. This will be supported by robust mechanisms that provide accurate and consistent real-time visibility of project or programme performance to enable timely decision making.

Enhance Capability

Train and coach individuals involved in project delivery in public and private sector organisations to increase their knowledge of the discipline and its application. This will equip them with the skills, knowledge, and tools needed to perform project work which will not only ensure the successful delivery of projects but will also upskill the project delivery workforce.

Deliver Change

Driving the adoption and implementation of evaluation processes to determine objectively the relevance, effectiveness, efficiency, sustainability and impact of deliverables post project completion. The focus here is on analysis of the progress made towards the achievement of the committed outcomes through mechanisms that measure the real impact of projects/programmes.
