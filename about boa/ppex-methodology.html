PPEX Methodology

Project Portfolio Execution (PPEX™) Methodology

PPEx™ has been specifically conceived to support companies operating in increasingly challenging environments. With stakeholders expecting more, organisations must cope with growing constraints on resources and funds. There’s a low margin for error, so effective and efficient management of investment required to meet your strategic objectives, is now more important than ever.

Investing in the wrong projects, which do not contribute to strategy, can be costly and time consuming, limiting the flow of capital to the areas it is needed most. On the other hand, the ability to select the right projects is not always a guarantee of value-add, unless these are executed properly. How do organisations ensure that the issues encountered in project and programme delivery are addressed so that anticipated benefits are realised? PPEx™ helps its clients address these challenges by:

  • Providing mature processes and frameworks that help select the right projects, which are critical to delivering solutions and innovations required to move your organisation forward.
  • Enabling you build the capability required to effectively deliver your projects and achieve expected outcomes through best practice standards and techniques.
